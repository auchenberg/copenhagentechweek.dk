About Copenhagen Techfest

All about Technology
All over Copenhagen
All of September

Fast-growing startups, novel innovations, buzzing co-working spaces, rise in investments, experienced forces and lots of talent. Copenhagen is racing to become the next tech hub with lots to offer.

At the brand new Copenhagen Tech Fest, we are celebrating this. For the first time in Denmark, we are uniting the startup community, aligning events, building a stage and putting the best ideas on it. Meet the talent, see the innovations and feel the energy, when we share the latest and put the future Danish tech stars on a pedestal.
