Fast-growing startups, novel innovations, buzzing co-working spaces, rise in investments, experienced forces and lots of talent. With big news and hectic energy in the startup community, Copenhagen is racing to become the next tech hub.

At the brand new Copenhagen TechFest, we are celebrating this. For the first time in Denmark, we are uniting the startup community, aligning events, building a stage and putting the best ideas on it. Meet the talent, see the innovations and feel the energy, when we share the latest and put the future stars on a pedestal.
